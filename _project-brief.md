# Project Brief — ukalcoholrehab.com (Site 3)

## Network Context
This site is part of a seven-site microsite network
supporting The Wellbourne Clinic.

Hub site: https://thewellbourneclinic.co.uk/

## This Site's Purpose
National and location-led alcohol rehab discovery guide.

The page approaches alcohol rehab through place: whether to
stay close to home, travel for privacy and distance, and how to
compare treatment settings across the UK. It combines a broad
recovery journey with featured city and regional references.

## Current Search Snippet
### Title
UK Alcohol Rehab | Alcohol Rehab Across the UK

### Meta Description
A clear, compassionate guide to alcohol rehab in the UK.
Explore residential alcohol rehab, treatment centres, costs,
locations and confidential support.

## Tone
- Highly compassionate, reflective and hope-led
- Editorial and human rather than clinical or transactional
- Uses short, emotionally resonant headings and recovery imagery
- Reassures users that treatment is available regardless of
  postcode and that travelling for care can be valid
- Practical beneath the softer presentation, especially around
  cost, length of stay, therapy, aftercare and family involvement
- Non-pressuring and confidential

## This Site's Content Angle
This site owns the geographical and setting-choice angle for
alcohol rehab in the UK.

Its distinctive content focuses on:

- Alcohol rehab across the UK
- Whether location matters when choosing rehab
- Staying near home versus travelling for treatment
- Residential treatment as protected time and space
- Featured locations and regions
- Family access, privacy and aftercare continuity
- How to evaluate a treatment environment

It still explains the overall recovery journey, but the central
editorial device is place, distance and choosing where recovery
should begin.

## Important
The current site is national/location-themed rather than a true
local SEO architecture. The six place names are presented as
featured cards on one page. Its strongest ownership is "alcohol
rehab across the UK" and location-choice intent, with potential
overlap with site 2 on the broad term "alcohol rehab UK".
